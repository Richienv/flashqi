Here’s a more varied and challenging quiz format, including multiple-choice, fill-in-the-blanks, and sentence-creation questions, while still staying in context of your lesson vocabulary:

Lesson 1

1. Fill in the Hanzi:
“One hundred is 一 ___。”
Answer: ________

2. Multiple-choice (Choose correct Hanzi):
“The word for ‘mouth’ in Chinese is:”
	•	A) 大
	•	B) 口
	•	C) 白

3. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	女 (nǚ - female/woman)
Example answer: 我是女学生。

Lesson 2

4. Multiple-choice (Tone recognition):
“The tone for 妈 (ma, meaning ‘mother’) is:”
	•	A) mā (1st tone)
	•	B) má (2nd tone)
	•	C) mǎ (3rd tone)

5. Fill in the correct Pinyin:
“The Chinese language is called 汉语. Its correct Pinyin is ___.”
Answer: __________

6. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	难 (nán - difficult)
Example answer: 汉语不太难。

Lesson 3

7. Multiple-choice (Vocabulary):
“The correct Hanzi for the language ‘Japanese’ is:”
	•	A) 德语
	•	B) 日语
	•	C) 法语

8. Fill in the blank (Hanzi):
“我要去___寄信。” (I want to go to the post office to send a letter.)
Answer: __________

9. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	钱 (qián - money)
Example answer: 我明天去银行取钱。

Lesson 4

10. Multiple-choice (Identify the correct day):
“昨天星期三，今天是:”
	•	A) 星期四
	•	B) 星期二
	•	C) 星期天

11. Fill in the correct Hanzi:
“How many (多少/几) students do you have? 你有___个学生？”
Answer: __________

12. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	学校 (xuéxiào - school)
Example answer: 明天我不去学校。

Lesson 5

13. Fill in the correct Hanzi:
“Please sit. 请___。”
Answer: __________

14. Multiple-choice (polite expression):
“An appropriate polite response to 谢谢 is:”
	•	A) 你好
	•	B) 不客气
	•	C) 再见

15. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	身体 (shēntǐ - body/health)
Example answer: 您身体好吗？

Lesson 6

16. Fill in correct Hanzi:
“What country are you from? 你是哪___人？”
Answer: __________

17. Multiple-choice (Hanzi Recognition):
“The Hanzi for ‘surname’ is:”
	•	A) 姓
	•	B) 名
	•	C) 字

18. Create a sentence in Hanzi using the word:
	•	高兴 (gāoxìng - happy)
Example answer: 认识你我很高兴。

This format ensures variety and challenges students to actively recall and use vocabulary contextually.
